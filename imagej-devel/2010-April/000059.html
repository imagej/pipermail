As I mentioned recently, I would like to use Maven for structuring projects,
modules and dependencies for ImageJ. Ideally, this would include imglib as
well. I like the breakdown of core vs. algorithm, and of course tests are

bsd-algorithms vs. gpl-algorithms, which also makes sense if needed.

Unfortunately, I will not have time to pursue a Maven-based directory

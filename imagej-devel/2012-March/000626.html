> The ImageJ User and Developer Conference 2012 follows the concepts of the
> conferences organised in Luxembourg in 2006, 2008, 2010 and 2012. The
> event offers
> workshops, in which the users will be able to improve their knowledge and
> usage of the
> software. These workshops will cover "beginner" and "user" topics as well
> as "advanced"
> topics focusing on the "developer" level. We try to specify the
> pre-knowledge of each
> workshop as clear as possible. For the workshops, you should bring your
> own laptop, to be
> able to test the course material on the own hardware. If this is not
> possible, workshop
> computers are available.
>
> Besides these workshops, shorter presentations are planned. These oral
> presentations are
> limited to 15 min presentations (12 min + 3 min questions), case studies,
> projects, plugins
> and solutions to common problems will be presented. The submitted and
> accepted
> presentations will be grouped into topics, to introduce these topics a
> keynote will be given
> by invited talks. We reduced the time frame duration to address the demand
> to have only
> single session to give everybody the possibility to see all presentations.
> We ask you to
> transfer the copyright for publishing of the accepted articles have to be
> granted to the
> Centre de Recherche Public Henri Tudor.
>
> Scientific posters and an expo area will accompany these presentations.
> Compared to
> recent years, we will have more space for this part of the conference.
> We are convinced that this conference will be again a "meeting place" for
> the community
> to discuss the usage and the development with and of ImageJ. We remain
> open to every
> additional idea for the conference. The format of the conference is not
> fixed, it can be
> modeled by the ImageJ Community. Just drop us a message.
>

Gabriel's nice points brought up  the issue of bias in counting pixels (a common problem in image processing applications is that when turning a shape with curves into pixels, the pixels are under-counted on the LHS and over-counted on the RHS, IJ2's bug appears to be have a top/down orientation).  This problem is present in IJ2, but has been solved in IJ1 (I think was maybe even solved not too long ago in IJ1).  Here is an example:

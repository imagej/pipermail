Hi Curtis,

have you tried to help javac to infer T by calling the method with an
explicit parameter?  E.g.:

ImageOpener.<ExplicitType>openImg(...);

In the context of Dataset which anyway erases T to RealType, you may
also just open ImgPlus without T, suppress the warnings and on demand
cast back.

Best,
Stephan

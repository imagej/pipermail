I am using the latest version of Fiji on multiple computers (both Windows PC
and Mac). I just noticed a bug: The Edit -> Options -> Look and Feel setting
change will never be saved. 

On the Mac, the default is "Mac OS X', on the PC, it's "Metal". Once Fiji is
restarted, the Look and Feel will always go back to the default, never remember
the changed valued. 

I tried to changed where the Fiji.app folder is located, and changed the
read-only attribute of the folder, nothing worked. 

Other settings are retained fine from session to session. But Look and feel
never remembers the change.

Hi Steven,

There are two things that I would like you to try:

1) Opening your data with "File > Import > Bio-Formats".
If this fails, then the issue is probably with Bio-Formats and you should
submit a bug using their issue tracking guidelines:
http://www.openmicroscopy.org/site/support/bio-formats5/about/bug-reporting.html

2) Opening again with "File > Open", but once with "Edit > Options > ImageJ2...
> Use SCIFIO when opening files" enabled, and once disabled.

If the Bio-Formats importer test looks good, but it fails with SCIFIO, then
please upload your .dm3 dataset either as an attachment here, or via "Help >
Upload Sample Image" if you would like the data to remain private. That will
allow us to reproduce the issue and investigate further.

Thanks!

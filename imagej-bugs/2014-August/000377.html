--- Comment #1 from Curtis Rueden <ctrueden at wisc.edu> ---
You can fall back to an older version of Bio-Formats by downloading one of the
"Life-Line" versions of Fiji from the Downloads page.

The 2013-July version comes with Bio-Formats circa 4.4.9.
The 2014-June version comes with Bio-Formats 5.0.2.

In any case, please report this bug on the OME Users mailing list, which is the
best place to report Bio-Formats issues.

http://lists.openmicroscopy.org.uk/mailman/listinfo/ome-users/

Sample files less than ~500MB can be uploaded to the OME QA system:

http://qa.openmicroscopy.org.uk/qa/upload/

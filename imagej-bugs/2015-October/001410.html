--- Comment #1 from Curtis Rueden <ctrueden at wisc.edu> ---
It is not recommended to install Fiji to Program Files.
  http://imagej.net/Downloads#Installation

Instead, unpack the distribution somewhere you control, such as 'My Documents'
or 'Desktop' in your home directory.

(As an aside: in the future, we will likely have an installer for ImageJ that
installs into 'Program Files' like other software does, and sets the
permissions appropriately. See https://github.com/imagej/imagej/issues/72 for
details.)

By the way, it looks like you are running a _really_ old version of Fiji! You
might want to try updating. But back up your existing installation first -- it
will be a big update. ;-)

End users don’t like macro languages in Cell biology imaging facilities. This
isn’t a problem I personally have, it is a problem 200 of my end users have

bit unsigned tiffs. Since I’m aware of several facilities worldwide which use
Coolsnap HQ2 and other cameras that acquire in 14 bit I’m not sure why this
isn’t implemented in the GUI. Particularly as 15 bit, which is a workaround for
fixed pattern noise of Flash4 sCMOS cameras, is.

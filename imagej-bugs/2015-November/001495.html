--- Comment #1 from Wayne Rasband <wsr at nih.gov> ---
The scale bar is an overlay so you need to "flatten" the image before saving in
TIFF format by pressing shift-f (Image>Overlay>Flatten). Or, when creating a
scale bar, uncheck "Overlay" in the dialog box.

Hi Phil,

 I'm sorry to hear you're having trouble downloading this manual.

 Unfortunately, there is not much we can do to help you directly. I was able to
view and download the manual without problem. So it sounds like there could be
a problem with your acrobat reader, browser, etc... but that this isn't an
issue with Fiji itself.

For the purposes of this bug tracker, I'm going to close this as "invalid"
because of the scope of the problem (not being a Fiji bug).

However, I would recommend you try some of the following:

* Try downloading from http://imagej.net/plugins/fraclac/fraclac-manual.pdf
* Look through the Adobe troubleshooting guides, e.g.
http://helpx.adobe.com/acrobat/kb/cant-view-pdf-web.html

http://imagej.net/list.html

Good luck!
